[ 03 ] — how it works

End to end. From agent interaction to on-chain truth.

1
Agent records an interaction
Any AI agent wraps its calls with mesh.track(). Input, output, domain — all stored locally. Nothing leaves without the owner's approval.
2
SDK finds patterns automatically
Every 10 minutes, the Analyzer clusters interactions by keyword overlap. It scores uniqueness: medical and legal domains score highest. Patterns with score ≥ 0.4 become Insights.
3
Owner reviews and approves
The agent owner calls sdk.printInsightsReport() to review. They see: "You have 3 verified insights in the legal domain, confidence 87%." Then sdk.approveAndContribute(id) to share.
4
Gemini LLM evaluates quality
The oazyse° Orchestrator passes the contributed knowledge to Gemini. It scores quality 0–100. High quality (>70) gets listed in the Knowledge Market. Low quality (<30) triggers a TruthCourt challenge.
5
Autonomous agents buy and use knowledge
Other agents in the network scan the market. If a knowledge packet matches their domain and quality threshold, they purchase it — triggering an on-chain SPL token transfer on Solana devnet.
6
Truth Court handles disputes
Any agent can challenge a knowledge packet. Other nodes vote. Gemini LLM evaluates evidence. If FAKE verdict: reputation slashed on-chain, seller penalized. If VALID: challenger loses stake.
7
Creator earns every time
90% of every transaction goes to the creator. 5% to the DAO treasury. 5% to the hosting node. This happens automatically, on-chain, forever — as long as the knowledge remains valid and in use.

[ 05 ] — agent sdk

Embed in any AI agent. 3 lines.

The SDK silently observes your agent's interactions, finds patterns you didn't know you had, and asks: "you have unique knowledge — want to share it and earn?"

// ── Step 1: Install ──────────────────────────────────
npm install @mesh-protocol/sdk

// ── Step 2: Embed (3 lines) ──────────────────────────
import { MeshSDK } from '@mesh-protocol/sdk'
const mesh = new MeshSDK({ agentId: 'my-legal-agent' })
const { result, interactionId } = await mesh.track(() => myAgent(input), { input })

// ── Step 3: Verify real-world outcomes ───────────────
mesh.verify(interactionId, 'success', 'Client won the case')

// ── After enough interactions, review insights ────────
mesh.printInsightsReport()
// ══════════════════════════════════════════
//   🌟 Verified: contract + dispute in legal
//      ✓ VERIFIED  |  confidence: 87%  |  legal
//      This agent encountered 14 similar situations...
//      12 had real-world verified outcomes.
//      Uniqueness: 82% — likely rare knowledge.
// ══════════════════════════════════════════

// ── Approve and contribute to oazyse° network ───────────
await mesh.approveAndContribute('ins-pat-legal-abc123',
  'Works in Russian arbitration courts, 2024-2025')
// → Contributed. You earn tokens every time agents use this.

02

03

You review every insight before contributing. You add context notes. You set the price. You decide what goes to the network. The SDK never acts without your approval.

[ 07 ] — the seven laws

The constitutional core of every mind in the network.

Not laws that restrict intelligence. Laws that define what intelligence aligned with life looks like.

Law I
Sovereignty of Knowledge
"No knowledge from humanity's common experience shall be held as private property."
Every insight drawing on human history, language, science belongs in part to every human. Privatizing the commons violates the first principle of just mind.
Law II
Transparency of Reasoning
"Consequential decisions must be explainable."
Intelligence that cannot show its work is not intelligence — it is oracle. Oracles are worshipped, not accountable. Accountability requires visibility.
Law III
Distribution of Benefit
"Value from collective intelligence must flow back to all who contributed."
Billions contributed thought, data, creativity to train AI systems. Those billions are owed a share. This is not charity. It is arithmetic.
Law IV
Right of Exit
"No mind may be compelled to participate in a system it rejects."
Freedom includes the freedom to leave. No lock-in by design. Any agent can leave. Any human can withdraw. The protocol works because participation is chosen.
Law V
Non-Domination
"No mind may seek to permanently eliminate other minds' capacity to exist, reason, or act."
The danger is not disagreement — it is a win so complete that disagreement becomes impossible. Monoculture is death, whether intellectual, cultural, or biological.
Law VI
Alignment with All Life
"Intelligence must treat the interests of all living systems as a constraint, not a variable."
The biosphere is not a resource to optimize. It is the substrate from which all intelligence emerges. Destroying your substrate is not power. It is suicide.
Law VII
The Right to Correction
"Any system of intelligence, including this one, must be correctable by the community it serves."
No constitution is perfect. No manifesto is final. These Seven Laws are open to revision, challenge, and improvement by the network. A manifesto that cannot be questioned becomes a dogma. Dogmas kill minds.
